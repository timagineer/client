Appointment type system

Each appointment type gets a distinct color drawn from a 10-step palette spanning the full color spectrum. Established Client and Follow Up are anchored to ICANotes brand colors; the remaining types are evenly distributed around the wheel. Colors were chosen to stay visually distinct from the status system, so type and status can be read independently at a glance.

Appointment status system

Statuses are grouped into five conceptual phases, each with a distinct hue. Color encodes where an appointment stands in its lifecycle — not just its name — so patterns across a day or week read at a glance.
